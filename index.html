Your coaches

More than a school. A community.

Surf At was born in 2013 at Praia das Maçãs, out of two people's love of the sea. Gonçalo and Pedro grew up surfing, took different paths, then came back to do the thing they love most — together. What began as a small project grew into a community, in and out of the water. You learn from the founders and the crew they've trained, all certified by the Portuguese Surfing Federation.

Good to know

Questions, answered

I've never surfed. Is that ok?

More than ok — it's most of who we teach. Beginners start in small groups with certified coaches and the right waves. You'll likely stand up on day one.

What should I bring?

Just swimwear, a towel and sunscreen. Board and wetsuit are included every session.

Which languages do you coach in?

English, Portuguese, German and French — whatever makes you comfortable in the water.

Do you run all year?

Yes. Daily sessions through summer, flexible weekend and after-school programs through the cooler months.

Can you take a group or an event?

Absolutely — birthdays, stags, team trips. Tell us the group and the vibe and we'll build it, catering included.
